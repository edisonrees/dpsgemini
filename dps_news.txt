:NANI: Project Immortality Field :NANI: 
 In late 2024, amidst Operation Overflow, DPS members squeezed their brains in search for the most efficient ways of removing blocks en masse, and thus, Project Immortality Field was born. Based on an illegal status effect bypass which implied creepers and splash potions, DPS member Vlesmentie came up with a better way of performing this bypass: storing splash potions in stasis chambers. Soon, Kurtz built a functional design for a massive stash of potions, so we could attain immortality any time we wanted to, to enslave withers to remove obsidian at spawn.

Creating the field in safe coords took only two weeks, and the project served its great purpose during almost 6 months.

Other players like MrDarkEye and Kozy reverse-engineered our bypass, but we're the first ones to use and abuse it. Once Overflow was done, the massive stash of stasis chambers was emptied and all the modules were unloaded, so that it didn't fall on ill-intended hands. Now, all the exploits that made it possible have been thoroughly patched with no chance at bypassing, and thus, we are making this public knowledge for the flex and giggles :richpepe:

Nerd stats
The field had 5760 modules.
The build process was completed in less than two weeks, in December 2024.
The structure was comprised by 400,000+ blocks, most of them glass, and was litematica-printed for max efficiency.
It remained operational for close to 6 months.
Mostly used for resistance potions (immortality, hence the name), but also haste 255 & speed 255 potions.
